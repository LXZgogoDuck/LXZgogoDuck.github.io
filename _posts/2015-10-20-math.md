[//]: # (This theme supports rendering beautiful math in inline and display modes using [MathJax 3]&#40;https://www.mathjax.org/&#41; engine. You just need to surround your math expression with `$$`, like `$$ E = mc^2 $$`. If you leave it inside a paragraph, it will produce an inline expression, just like $$ E = mc^2 $$.)

[//]: # ()
[//]: # (To use display mode, again surround your expression with `$$` and place it as a separate paragraph. Here is an example:)
